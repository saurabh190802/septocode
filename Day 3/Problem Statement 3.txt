Given that your semester results are out amd you have received the credits of each subject. Frame a program to input your credit of each subject in a line in form of a string separated by commas. And it will show all credits individually along with total credits.

Input Format: 
String: Various integers separated by commas.

Output Format:
All the integers printed line by line

Constraints: 
The length of str is less than 8*10^5

Sample Input: 3,4,5,2

Sample Output:    
3
4
5
2
